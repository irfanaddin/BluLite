Our Story

BluLite Web Solutions was founded by Irfan Hamid, a UC San Diego graduate with a degree in Cognitive Science specializing in User Experience Design. Driven by a passion for helping businesses build better online experiences, Irfan started BluLite to combine strategic thinking with modern design and development.

We believe that every business deserves a digital presence that is user-friendly, visually stunning, and built to convert visitors into loyal customers. Our focus is on delivering custom websites that meet real business needs and exceed client expectations.

Our Mission

To empower businesses by designing websites that are both beautiful and strategically effective, making an impact where it matters most.

Our Vision

To be a trusted digital partner that businesses rely on to enhance their brand presence and grow their online success.
